Pasta Salad

Description

Pasta salad with pepperoni, tomatoes, and provolone cheese. Good side-dish for picnics or dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fusilli (spiral) pasta
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 pound salami, cubed
  • 1/4 pound sliced pepperoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 (10 ounce) can of black olives, drained
  • 1 (4 ounce) jar of pimentos, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle of italian salad dressing

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. cook fusilli pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Combine pasta with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green onions, olives, and pimentos in a large bowl. Pour in salad dressing; toss to coat.
  4. Enjoy!
